Holistic Care for Mental Well-being

Andtil Health Care offers compassionate and comprehensive care for eating disorders and mental health. Our team, including Mental Health Professionals and Nutritionists, collaborates to address psychological and nutritional aspects for holistic recovery.

Psychological Evaluations

Thorough assessments to understand the root causes and triggers of eating disorders.

Nutritional Counseling

Work with our experts to establish a balanced and supportive approach to nutrition for mental well-being.

Integrated Therapies

Collaborative care that combines psychological support with nutritional strategies for a holistic approach to recovery.
