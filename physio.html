Physio

Physiotherapy Services at
Andtil Health Care

Welcome to Andtil Health Care's Physiotherapy Services, where our dedicated team of experienced physiotherapists is committed to enhancing your physical well-being. We offer a range of specialized services to address various health concerns and promote optimal mobility and function.

Sports Injuries Rehabilitation

Recover from sports-related injuries with our specialized sports rehabilitation programs. Our physiotherapists work closely with athletes to facilitate a safe and effective return to peak performance.

2. Pain Management (Muscle & Joint Pains):

Experience relief from muscle and joint pains through our comprehensive pain management programs. Our physiotherapy techniques focus on reducing pain, improving flexibility, and enhancing overall joint function.

3. Pre & Post Rehab for Surgeries:

Prepare for surgery with pre-rehabilitation programs designed to optimize your physical condition. Post-surgery, our rehabilitation plans aid in a smooth recovery, promoting strength and mobility.

4. Spine Care:

Address spinal issues and improve spinal health with our specialized spine care programs. Our physiotherapists employ targeted techniques to alleviate pain, enhance flexibility, and improve overall spine function.

5. Neuro Rehab:

Experience personalized neurological rehabilitation that focuses on enhancing mobility, balance, and coordination for individuals with neurological conditions. Our neuro rehab programs are tailored to each patient's unique needs.

6. Pediatric Physiotherapy:

Support the developmental needs of children with our pediatric physiotherapy services. Our specialists work with young patients to address motor skills, coordination, and other developmental concerns.

7. Women's Health - Antenatal / Postnatal Care:

Ensure a healthy pregnancy and postpartum recovery with our specialized women's health physiotherapy services. We offer support for antenatal and postnatal care, addressing specific concerns related to pregnancy and childbirth.

8. Geriatric Problems:

Enhance the quality of life for older adults with our geriatric physiotherapy services. Our programs focus on maintaining and improving mobility, reducing pain, and addressing age-related health concerns.
